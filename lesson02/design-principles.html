Hick’s Law - Simple Menu

Amazon categorizing choice, the user is not overwhelmed. We should group menu items into high-level categories – the user can go on further into the menus more confidently when they’re categorized in a sensible, straight-forward way.

The Rule of Thirds

The rule of thirds grid gives you the chance to give your graphic design a perfectly symmetrical appearance. If your design is imbalanced, it throws off the entire look. Using a rule of thirds grid helps you maintain good balance while still keeping things asymmetrical.

Company Folders reference

White Space and Clean Design

White space is a great tool to balance design elements and better organize content to improve the visual communication experience. The white space is the real star of the show, working between the words and the pictures. It keeps each page from looking busy.

The Washington Post is an excellent example because the website tries to imitate the journal paper.
